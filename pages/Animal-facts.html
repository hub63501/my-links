How many bees live in a hive?

There can be up to 80,000 honeybee workers.

There is one adult queen bee. She can live for as long as five years. The adult worker bees tend to live about 6 weeks.

What creature shoots blood from it's eyes?

If a horned lizard is attacked it can increase blood pressure in veins in it's eye socket until it bursts. Blood can squirt up to 122cm and the predictor who gets squirted thinks it's poisonous and therefore leaves the lizard alone.

What is the Biggest Dragonfly?

The largest dragonfly is the south american giant damselfly.

They have a wingspan of up to 19cm with a body of 12cm long. However there were fossils of dragonflies that had wingspans of 75cm. Which means they would have been a similar size to a small dog.

How good is a Tigers eye sight in the dark?

A Tiger can see six times better than a human in the dark.
